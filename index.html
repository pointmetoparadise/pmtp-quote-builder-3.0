Setup Guide one-time setup only
Do these steps once. After that, just fill in the builder and copy the prompt for every new quote.
Part 1 — Web3Forms (emails you every time a client submits their selection)
1
Go to web3forms.com. Click "Get Started Free."
2
Enter your work email and click "Get your Access Key."
Use the email where you want to receive client selections.
3
Check your inbox for a Web3Forms confirmation email. Click the confirmation link inside it.
Check spam if you don't see it within 2 minutes.
4
Your Access Key is displayed — looks like: 7b40d437-1004-48e2-9031-171a8b0ef4f1. Copy it and paste into the Agent Info section above. It never changes.
5
Log in at app.web3forms.com anytime to see all submissions in a dashboard.
Done. One access key works for all your quotes forever.
Part 2 — GitHub (hosts your live client-facing quote pages for free)
1
Go to github.com. Click "Sign up" and create a free account. Any username is fine — clients never see it.
2
Click the "+" icon top right → "New repository."
3
Name it pmtp-quotes (lowercase, no spaces). Select "Public." Click "Create repository."
Must be Public — Private repos cannot use free GitHub Pages hosting.
4
Click "Settings" in the top menu. In the left sidebar click "Pages."
5
Under Branch select "main" → leave folder as "/ (root)" → click "Save."
6
Wait 2–3 minutes. Refresh the Pages screen. Green banner: "Your site is live at https://[username].github.io/pmtp-quotes/"
Paste this URL into Agent Info above. Every HTML file you upload will be live here.
Done. Never create another repo — just keep uploading files to this one forever.
Part 3 — Uploading a Quote Page (every new quote)
1
After Claude builds your HTML file, download it to your computer.
2
Go to github.com/[yourusername]/pmtp-quotes.
3
Click "Add file" → "Upload files."
4
Drag your HTML file into the upload box, or click "choose your files."
5
Click the green "Commit changes" button.
6
Wait 2–3 minutes. Live at: https://[username].github.io/pmtp-quotes/[filename].html
Replacing a file: Upload with the same filename. Wait 3 min then add ?v=2 to the URL to force browser reload.
404 error? Wait 3 more minutes. Check that the filename in the URL exactly matches what you uploaded — capital letters matter.
All quotes in one repo. You never need to create another repo.
Part 4 — Test Before Sending to a Client
1
Open your live quote URL in a browser.
2
Select a hotel — the running total should update immediately.
3
Check and uncheck add-on items — the total should update live each time.
4
Fill in your own name and email. Click Submit. Check your inbox — Web3Forms email arrives in 1–2 minutes with the full selection summary and total.
5
Everything good? Send the client their PDF. The last page has a gold button linking directly to the selection page.
